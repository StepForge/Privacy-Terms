Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how StepFit (“the App”) processes information. StepFit is developed by Iman Mirzazadegan. The App is designed to keep most data on your device, while offering optional integrations such as Health Connect and optional cloud backup.

1. Who we are

Data controller: Iman Mirzazadegan (independent developer).
App name: StepFit

2. At a glance

Stored on your device

Step history, water intake, sleep sessions, app preferences, theme choice, reminders, and (if enabled) App Lock configuration are stored locally using Android app storage.

Optional external services

If you enable them, StepFit can interact with Health Connect (to read selected health records) and Firebase services (for cloud backup / restore and crash reporting).

3. Data we process

3.1 Activity and step data

StepFit can read your step count from device sensors (such as the Android step counter sensor). The App may also store step history by day, and may store hourly step snapshots to support hourly charts and history screens.

  • Daily step totals (by date)
  • Optional hourly step snapshots (by date/hour)
  • Derived metrics shown in the UI (for example distance and calories estimates)

3.2 Sleep data

StepFit includes a Sleep screen. Sleep sessions may be created manually or imported from Health Connect if you grant the required permission. StepFit stores sleep sessions locally for your history view.

  • Sleep session times (start/end), total duration, quality score
  • Source label (for example “manual” or “health_connect”)
  • Optional sleep stages (when available from Health Connect)

3.3 Hydration (water) data

StepFit includes a Water Reminder feature and daily water intake tracking. The App can store:

  • Daily water totals (ml) per date
  • Reminder schedule preferences (interval and active hours)

3.4 Profile data you provide

StepFit can store optional profile fields you enter to personalize the experience:

  • Name / nickname
  • Gender, birth date (used to derive age), height, weight
  • Preferred distance unit (km/mi)

3.5 App Lock and security information

If you enable App Lock, StepFit can store locally:

  • Whether App Lock is enabled
  • A 4‑digit PIN (used to unlock the app)
  • Whether biometric unlock is allowed (biometric data itself is not accessed or stored by StepFit)
  • Optional security questions and answers used for PIN reset
  • Lock timeout preference and session unlock state

3.6 Settings and preferences

StepFit stores preferences such as daily goal, reminder time, and theme selection to operate the App.

3.7 Cloud backup and restore (optional)

StepFit offers an optional cloud backup feature. When you use Sync & Backup, StepFit can upload a backup to cloud storage using Firebase services.

Based on the current backup design, the cloud backup is intended to include:

  • Your step history (daily step totals)
  • Selected non-sensitive settings (for example unit, daily step goal, theme mode, notification time, auto-sync toggle)

The backup is not intended to include sensitive profile fields (such as height/weight/birth date), nor App Lock secrets (PIN and related lock settings).

3.8 Diagnostics and crash reports

StepFit may use crash reporting tools (such as Firebase Crashlytics) to understand stability issues. Crash reports typically include device/app information and error traces. StepFit does not intentionally include your step history, profile, or health records in crash reports.

4. How we use the data

  • To show your progress (steps, goal completion, history, widgets)
  • To run reminders and notifications (daily reminder, hydration reminders, inactivity prompts)
  • To power sleep and water insights
  • To enable App Lock (when turned on)
  • To perform cloud backup and restore (only if you use that feature)
  • To improve stability and fix bugs (crash diagnostics)

5. Where your data is stored

5.1 On-device storage

Most StepFit data is stored locally in the app’s private storage on your device (for example using Room and DataStore). This data is not shared with other apps unless you explicitly enable a system feature that does so.

5.2 Health Connect

If you enable Health Connect integration, StepFit can read the Health Connect data types you allow. You control access and can revoke it at any time from Health Connect settings.

5.3 Cloud backup storage

If you use cloud backup, StepFit stores a backup using Firebase services associated with your authentication state (for example a Google sign-in flow). The backup is used solely to let you restore supported data on the same or another device.

6. Sharing and third parties

StepFit does not sell your personal data. StepFit may share limited data with service providers only when you use features that rely on them:

  • Google Health Connect (if you connect it) to read permitted health records.
  • Firebase (Google) for authentication, cloud backup storage, and crash diagnostics.
  • Your email app (if you choose to send feedback) to draft and send an email message.

7. Permissions

StepFit may request permissions needed for app features, such as:

  • Activity Recognition (step counting)
  • Notifications (reminders)
  • Health Connect permissions (steps/distance/calories/weight/sleep), only if you enable Health Connect features
  • Exact alarms (to schedule reminders reliably on some Android versions)

8. Data retention and deletion

  • You can clear local StepFit data from the in-app Privacy & Security screen (Clear Local Data).
  • Uninstalling the app removes StepFit’s locally stored data from your device.
  • If you enabled Health Connect, Health Connect data retention is controlled via Health Connect settings.
  • If you used cloud backup, backups may remain in cloud storage until overwritten by a newer backup or removed.

9. Security

StepFit uses standard Android platform protections and stores data in the app’s private storage. No mobile system can guarantee absolute security; please use device-level protections (screen lock, OS updates) and enable App Lock if you want an extra layer inside StepFit.

10. Children’s privacy

StepFit is not designed specifically for children under 13. If you are a parent/guardian and want information removed, you can clear the app’s data or uninstall it.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. The latest version will be published at this page and may be referenced from within the app.
