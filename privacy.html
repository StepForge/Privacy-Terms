Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how StepForge (“StepForge”, “we”, “us”, or “the App”) processes information when you install or use the App. StepForge is developed and maintained by Iman Mirzazadegan (“Developer”).

StepForge is designed to be practical and privacy-respecting. Most activity and wellness data is stored locally on your device. Optional features such as Health Connect, Google account profile import, Firebase cloud backup/restore, and crash diagnostics may involve third-party services only when the related feature is enabled or used.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to StepForge distributed through official channels and covers features available in the App, including step tracking, progress dashboards, history/day detail, achievements, streaks, level/XP-style progress, profile personalization, goals, widgets, notifications, hydration, sleep, workouts, App Lock, Health Connect, feedback/support, debug exports, and Sync & Backup.

2. Summary

Local-first

Daily steps, progress, achievements, goals, water logs, sleep logs, workout summaries, profile preferences, and most settings are stored on your device by default.

Optional integrations

Health Connect, Google account profile import, Firebase cloud backup, and crash reporting are optional or feature-dependent. You can use StepForge without enabling Health Connect or cloud backup.

Important: StepForge does not sell your personal data and does not use advertising SDKs designed to track you across apps.

3. Data we process

3.1 Steps, progress, and activity data

StepForge can read step counts from device motion sensors and may store step totals to power Home, Progress, History, widgets, achievements, streaks, and goal visuals.

  • Daily step totals by date
  • Optional hourly step snapshots for charts and day detail
  • Derived estimates such as distance, calories, active minutes, progress score, averages, trends, heatmaps, and goal completion
  • Manual adjustments you make to steps or water values

Derived estimates are approximate and can vary based on device behavior, sensor accuracy, Health Connect data, and profile settings.

3.2 Profile and personalization data

StepForge can store optional profile and personalization fields you enter or approve, including:

  • Name or nickname
  • Optional profile picture or image URI
  • Gender, birth date/age, height, weight, and preferred units when provided
  • Level/XP-style progress, total points, streak summaries, premium status display, and achievement progress
  • Goal settings for steps, calories, active minutes, distance, water, and sleep

3.3 Google account profile import

If you connect a Google account, StepForge may receive basic account profile information such as your display name, email address, and profile photo URL from Google sign-in. StepForge shows a confirmation screen before using this information in your profile. You can save it, edit it, or decline it.

3.4 Achievements and sharing

StepForge tracks achievement unlock status, progress toward milestones, earned dates, share screenshots generated in the app, and related display data. Sharing is user-initiated: StepForge prepares the share image/content, and you choose whether and where to share it.

3.5 Hydration and sleep

StepForge can store hydration goals, water intake logs, reminder settings, sleep goals, manual sleep sessions, nap records, imported Health Connect sleep sessions, optional sleep stages, sleep quality scores, and sleep source labels such as manual or Health Connect.

3.6 Workouts and daily summaries

StepForge may store workout sessions or workout-derived summaries such as workout duration, distance, calories, steps, session type, timestamps, and daily snapshot totals.

3.7 App settings and security data

StepForge stores preferences such as theme, language, units, reminder schedules, widget settings, sync preferences, and App Lock settings. If App Lock is enabled, StepForge may store whether App Lock is enabled, a PIN or PIN-related configuration, timeout settings, biometric unlock preference, and recovery questions/answers if configured. Biometric data itself is handled by Android and is not received or stored by StepForge.

3.8 Health Connect data

If you enable Health Connect, StepForge may read or write only the health data types you grant, such as steps, distance, calories, weight, sleep sessions, and sleep stages. StepForge uses Health Connect to sync permitted data, show consistent history, and reduce duplicate records.

3.9 Cloud backup and restore

If you enable Sync & Backup, StepForge may upload supported categories to Firebase-based cloud storage so you can restore them later. Backups can include supported activity/history, goals, settings, achievements/progress metadata, hydration/sleep/workout summaries, and other supported StepForge state. Sensitive fields such as App Lock secrets are designed to be excluded unless clearly disclosed and required.

3.10 Feedback, support, debug logs, and crash diagnostics

If you send feedback or debug logs, the information you choose to send may include your message, email app metadata, app version, device/app diagnostics, timestamps, error traces, and selected StepForge state useful for troubleshooting. StepForge may also use Firebase Crashlytics to understand crashes and improve stability. Crash reports are not intended to include your full health history.

4. How we use data

  • Core tracking: show steps, distance, calories, active minutes, progress, and history.
  • Personalization: apply profile, units, theme, language, goals, water, and sleep preferences.
  • Insights: calculate trends, heatmaps, streaks, achievements, level/XP-style progress, daily snapshots, and summaries.
  • Reminders: schedule movement, hydration, sleep, and activity notifications.
  • Security: provide App Lock and related recovery options.
  • Integrations: sync permitted data with Health Connect or cloud backup when enabled.
  • Reliability: diagnose crashes, bugs, sync issues, and performance problems.

5. Where data is stored

5.1 On your device

Most StepForge data is stored locally in the app’s private storage, including Room databases, DataStore preferences, app files, and generated UI/cache data.

5.2 Health Connect

Health Connect records are managed by Health Connect and Android. StepForge can access only the data types you permit and only while permission remains granted.

5.3 Cloud storage

If Sync & Backup is enabled, supported backup data may be stored in Firebase services to provide restore and continuity. Backup data is not used for advertising.

6. Sharing and third parties

StepForge does not sell personal data. StepForge may interact with third-party services only to provide features you request or to maintain the app:

  • Google sign-in: optional account connection and profile information import.
  • Health Connect: optional health data sync based on permissions you grant.
  • Firebase services: authentication, cloud backup/restore, Firestore storage, analytics where configured, and crash diagnostics.
  • Your email/share apps: when you choose to send feedback, share achievements, or export debug information.

Third-party services are governed by their own policies and settings.

7. Permissions and controls

StepForge may request Android permissions such as Activity Recognition, Notifications, Exact Alarms, Health Connect permissions, and account-related permissions for Google sign-in. You can manage permissions in Android Settings, Health Connect settings, and StepForge settings.

8. Retention and deletion

Local data stays on your device until you delete it, clear app data, use in-app deletion controls where available, or uninstall StepForge. Health Connect data is managed through Health Connect. Cloud backups may remain until deleted, replaced, disconnected, or removed with assistance where in-app deletion is not yet available.

9. Security

StepForge uses local Android app storage, Android sandboxing, optional App Lock, and secure transport for cloud features. No system can guarantee absolute security; keep your device updated and use a device screen lock.

10. Children

StepForge is not designed specifically for children under 13. Parents or guardians can clear local data, uninstall the app, or contact the Developer for help with cloud backup data.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy to reflect new features, security improvements, platform requirements, or legal changes. The latest version will be published on this page.
