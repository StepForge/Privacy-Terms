Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how StepForge (“StepForge”, “we”, “us”, “our”, or “the App”) processes information when you install, access, or use the App. StepForge is developed and maintained by Iman Mirzazadegan (“Developer”).

StepForge is built as a privacy-conscious wellness app. The App is designed to keep most activity and wellness data on your device by default, while still offering optional integrations such as Health Connect, Google account sign-in, Firebase-based cloud backup/restore, crash diagnostics, and sharing features when you choose to use them.

Plain-language summary: StepForge does not sell your personal data, does not use advertising SDKs designed to track you across apps, and does not require a StepForge account for the core step tracking experience. Optional cloud, Health Connect, Google profile, and sharing features may process additional data only when used.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to StepForge distributed through official channels and to features available inside the App, including step tracking, Home dashboard, Progress dashboard, History and day detail, achievements, streaks, level/XP-style progress, profile personalization, goals, widgets, notifications, hydration, sleep and naps, workouts, App Lock, Health Connect, Google account profile import, feedback/support, debug exports, and Sync & Backup.

2. Responsible party

StepForge is developed and maintained by Iman Mirzazadegan. For privacy questions, data requests, or deletion help, contact stepforge0@gmail.com.

3. Privacy at a glance

Local-first design

Daily steps, history, goals, water logs, sleep logs, achievements, profile preferences, and most settings are stored locally on your device by default.

Optional cloud and integrations

Health Connect, Google sign-in/profile import, Firebase backup/restore, and crash diagnostics are optional or feature-dependent.

No sale of personal data

StepForge does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising SDKs designed for cross-app tracking.

User control

You control Android permissions, Health Connect access, Google account connection, backup options, and local deletion controls.

4.1 Step and sensor accuracy data

StepForge may compare device sensor output, Step Counter changes, Step Detector signals, timestamps, and validation logic to improve counting and reduce overcounting. Sensor-based activity metrics may still be inaccurate because Android hardware, phone placement, transport movement, vibration, and battery/background limits can affect results.

4.2 Manual edits and sync reconciliation

If you manually adjust steps, water, sleep, or other supported values, StepForge may store those edits locally and use source labels to avoid duplicate records when Health Connect or backup sync is enabled.

4.3 Generated images and sharing

Achievement share images and similar screenshots are generated locally for user-initiated sharing. StepForge does not automatically publish them. Your selected share target may process the shared content under its own terms and privacy policy.

5. Data sources

  • You: information you enter, edit, confirm, or share.
  • Your device: Android sensors, app storage, local notifications, widgets, and device settings.
  • Health Connect: records you explicitly allow StepForge to read or write.
  • Google account: basic sign-in/profile information if you connect Google and approve its use.
  • Firebase services: account, backup, Firestore, analytics where configured, and crash diagnostic services.

6. Why we process data

  • Core app operation: track steps, display progress, keep history, update widgets, and show daily totals.
  • Personalization: apply profile, theme, language, units, goals, hydration and sleep preferences.
  • Insights and motivation: calculate trends, achievements, streaks, daily summaries, personal records and goal completion.
  • Sync and continuity: import/export permitted Health Connect records and restore supported backup data.
  • Security: provide optional App Lock, PIN, biometric preference and recovery flows.
  • Communication: handle feedback, help requests, debug exports and support messages you initiate.
  • Reliability: detect crashes, improve performance, fix counting/sync bugs and protect data integrity.

7. Legal bases where required

Where data protection laws such as GDPR require a legal basis, StepForge generally relies on one or more of the following:

  • Performance of a service you request: providing core tracking, goals, history, widgets and profile features.
  • Consent: optional Health Connect access, Google sign-in/profile import, notifications, and optional cloud backup where applicable.
  • Legitimate interests: app security, crash diagnostics, fraud/abuse prevention, and reliability improvements that do not override your rights.
  • Legal obligations: where we must keep or disclose information to comply with applicable law.

This section is provided for transparency and does not limit rights that apply under mandatory local law.

8. Storage locations

8.1 On-device storage

Most StepForge data is stored in Android app-private storage using local databases, preferences, files and caches. Other apps cannot normally access this storage under Android sandboxing.

8.2 Health Connect

Health Connect data is managed by Android/Health Connect. StepForge can access only data types you allow and only while those permissions remain granted.

8.3 Cloud storage

If Sync & Backup is enabled, supported backup data may be stored using Firebase services. Cloud data is used to provide restore and continuity, not advertising.

8.4 Cache and generated files

StepForge may temporarily store generated images, debug exports, cached profile images, logs, or local UI resources. Temporary data may be cleared by the App, Android, or by clearing app storage.

9. Sharing and processors

StepForge does not sell personal data. Limited data may be processed by third-party services only when needed for features you use or to keep the App reliable:

  • Google Health Connect: health data access controlled by your permissions.
  • Google sign-in: optional account connection and basic profile import.
  • Firebase Authentication / Firestore / Cloud services: account-linked backup and restore when enabled.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: crash diagnostics and stability reports.
  • Google Play services: platform services, authentication, app distribution and security checks.
  • Email/share apps: feedback, debug export, or achievement sharing when initiated by you.

Third-party services have their own privacy policies and settings. StepForge cannot control how third-party apps process data after you choose to share or export it.

10. Permissions

StepForge may request permissions depending on enabled features:

  • Activity Recognition: to count steps and detect activity.
  • Notifications: to show reminders, activity prompts, goal updates and water reminders.
  • Exact alarms: to schedule reminders reliably on Android versions that restrict background work.
  • Health Connect permissions: to read/write only health data types you approve.
  • Internet/network: for optional sync, backup, sign-in, crash diagnostics and support features.
  • Biometric authentication: handled by Android if you enable biometric App Lock.

You can grant, deny or revoke permissions in Android Settings, Health Connect Settings, and StepForge settings.

11. Health Connect

Health Connect integration is optional. If enabled, StepForge may read or write permitted records such as steps, distance, total calories burned, weight, sleep sessions and sleep stages. StepForge uses source labels and reconciliation logic to reduce duplicate entries, for example distinguishing manual sleep/nap entries from imported Health Connect sessions.

Accuracy note: Health Connect data can come from other apps and devices. Differences between StepForge, Samsung Health, Google Fit, wearables, and Health Connect may occur because each source can use different sensors, filters, rounding and sync timing.

12. Sync and backup

Sync & Backup is optional and intended to help restore supported data after reinstall or device change. Backup categories may include activity history, goals, settings, achievements/progress metadata, hydration, sleep, workout summaries and other supported StepForge state.

Some data may be excluded for privacy or safety reasons, such as App Lock secrets, biometric data, temporary cache files, and other fields that should remain local. Backup availability, completeness and restore accuracy may depend on network connectivity, app version and Firebase availability.

13. Profile and Google account

When you connect Google, StepForge may show a confirmation dialog with available account information such as display name, email and profile photo. This information is not forced into your profile automatically; you can save it, edit it or decline it.

Profile fields are used for personalization, display, estimates and goals. You can edit profile and goal values inside the App.

14. Retention and deletion

  • Local data: remains until deleted in-app, cleared through Android app settings, or removed by uninstalling.
  • Health Connect: managed in Health Connect settings, including access permissions and record deletion.
  • Cloud backup: may remain until overwritten, disconnected, deleted through available controls, or removed with assistance.
  • Crash diagnostics: retained by Firebase/Google according to their service settings and retention periods.
  • Shared exports: controlled by the app or service you shared them to.

If an in-app delete option is not available for a specific cloud backup category, contact us for help.

15. Your rights and controls

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent for optional features.

  • Review and revoke Android permissions.
  • Review and revoke Health Connect permissions.
  • Edit profile and goal information in the App.
  • Disable sync/backup and disconnect account features where available.
  • Clear local data or uninstall the App.
  • Contact us for privacy questions or cloud backup deletion help.

16. Security

StepForge uses Android app-private storage, Android sandboxing, optional App Lock, secure transport for cloud services, and data-minimization practices where possible. No system can guarantee absolute security. Keep your device updated, use a device screen lock, and avoid sharing debug exports publicly.

17. Children

StepForge is not designed specifically for children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and want data removed, you can clear local data, uninstall the App, revoke Health Connect access, or contact us for cloud backup assistance.

18. International processing

If you use optional cloud, authentication, crash diagnostic, or support features, data may be processed on servers or systems located outside your country. Third-party providers may process data according to their own infrastructure and policies.

19. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect new features, platform requirements, security improvements, or legal changes. The latest version will be published on this page. Significant changes may also be referenced inside the App.
