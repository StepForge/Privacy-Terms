Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how StepFit (“the App”) handles information. StepFit is developed by Iman Mirzazadegan. The App is designed with a “local-first” approach: most of your data remains on your device. Optional features may use third-party platforms such as Health Connect and Firebase for cloud backup/restore and crash diagnostics.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to StepFit distributed through official app stores. It covers the features available in the App, including step tracking, goals, history, widgets, notifications, water reminders and tracking, sleep tracking, App Lock, and Sync & Backup.

2. Summary

  • Local-first: StepFit stores most data locally (Room + DataStore).
  • Permission-based: Health Connect access is optional and controlled by you.
  • Optional cloud: Sync & Backup uses Firebase services only if you use that feature.
  • Clear controls: You can clear local data from the app; cloud backup management is separate.

3. Data categories

3.1 Step and activity data

StepFit can process step counts from device sensors and (if enabled) Health Connect. The App may store:

  • daily step totals per date,
  • optional hourly step snapshots (for hourly charts and detail views),
  • derived estimates shown in the UI (distance, calories).

Derived metrics are estimates and depend on device sensors and your optional profile information.

3.2 Hydration data

StepFit can store your daily water intake totals (ml) and reminder schedule settings (interval, active hours). Water reminders are generated on your device.

3.3 Sleep data

StepFit includes sleep tracking. Sleep sessions may be created manually or imported from Health Connect (if permitted). Stored fields can include start/end time, duration, a quality score, and a “source” label (for example “manual” or “health_connect”). Some devices/records may provide sleep stages.

3.4 Profile data you enter

Optional profile data can include name, birth date, gender, height, weight, and unit preferences (km/mi). These help personalize the UI and improve estimates (for example distance/calories).

3.5 App Lock and security information

If you enable App Lock, StepFit stores local configuration (enabled flag, timeout, PIN, and optional security answers). Biometric unlock uses Android’s biometric system; StepFit does not store biometric templates.

3.6 Settings

StepFit stores settings such as daily goal, theme, notification time, and reminder configurations.

3.7 Cloud backup and restore (optional)

StepFit offers optional cloud backup/restore using Firebase services. When enabled/used, the App can upload a backup payload to your account’s storage path and later restore supported data.

  • Supported items can include step history and selected non-sensitive settings.
  • Sensitive fields (for example App Lock secrets and some profile fields) are intended to remain local-only.

3.8 Diagnostics

StepFit may use crash diagnostics (for example Firebase Crashlytics) to improve reliability. Crash reports typically include device/app technical information and error traces. StepFit does not intentionally include your step history or health records inside crash reports.

4. How we use data

  • To display your progress (steps, goals, rings, widgets, and history).
  • To schedule and show notifications (daily reminder, water reminders, inactivity prompts, goal alerts).
  • To provide sleep and hydration views.
  • To enforce App Lock if enabled.
  • To perform backup and restore when you choose to use cloud sync.
  • To debug stability issues (crash diagnostics).

5. Storage locations

5.1 On-device storage

StepFit stores most information in app-private storage. Other apps cannot access it unless your device is compromised or you explicitly share/export data.

5.2 Health Connect

When enabled, StepFit reads only the Health Connect record types you grant. Health Connect data is governed by Google’s policies and your Health Connect settings.

5.3 Firebase services

If you use Sync & Backup, StepFit uses Firebase for authentication and backup storage. If crash diagnostics are enabled, StepFit may send crash events to Firebase Crashlytics.

6. Sharing

StepFit does not sell personal information. StepFit may share limited data with service providers only when you use features that rely on them (Health Connect / Firebase / your email app for support).

7. Permissions

Permissions are used only for app functionality. Examples:

  • Activity Recognition (step counting).
  • Notifications (reminders).
  • Exact alarms (more reliable scheduling on some Android versions).
  • Health Connect permissions (only if you enable Health Connect features).

8. Clearing data and deletion

8.1 Clear Local Data

StepFit includes a “Clear Local Data” option in Privacy & Security. This clears locally stored preferences and local databases on your device.

8.2 Cloud backups and cloud deletion

Cloud backups (if you used Sync & Backup) are stored separately from local app storage. Clearing local data does not automatically delete cloud backups.

Planned improvement: A dedicated option to delete cloud backup data directly from within StepFit may be introduced in a future update to make cleanup more convenient and transparent.

9. Security

StepFit uses Android platform protections and well-known libraries. Network connections to third-party services (such as Firebase) are protected by standard encrypted transport provided by those platforms. You can enable App Lock for an extra in-app layer.

10. Children’s privacy

StepFit is not specifically designed for children under 13. If you want data removed, clear app data or uninstall StepFit.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. The latest version will be published here.
